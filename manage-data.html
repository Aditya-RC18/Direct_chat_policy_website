Manage & Delete Your Data

Last updated: July 2026

Direct Chat doesn't use accounts, sign-in, or profiles — there's nothing to "delete an account" for. Everything the app stores lives on your own device, and only leaves it if you turn on device sync.

Clear your data anytime, right on your device

1

Open Direct Chat and go to the Settings tab.

2

Tap Clear all data.

3

This permanently removes your history, favorites, templates, and groups from your device — instantly, with no waiting period.

If you've enabled sync

If you turned on device pairing/sync, clearing your data in-app also removes your synced copy tied to that pairing. There's no separate account to delete — the sync is just a private copy of your on-device data, linked to your pairing, not to an identity we manage.
